Orange History

Oranges originated in Asia in what is now called southeast China. Cultivated for at least 7,000 years in India and in China since 2,500 BCE and documented in China since 340 BCE, sweet orange (Citrus x sinensis) is a hybrid between pomelo (Citrus maxima) and mandarin (Citrus reticulata).

Benefits from orange:

  • Good source of vitamin C
  • Protects your cells from damage
  • Helps your body make collagen, a protein that heals wounds and gives you smoother skin
  • Makes it easier to absorb iron to fight anemia.

  2. Oranges may reduce your post-workout pain
  3. Oranges may reduce your risk of esophageal cancer
  4. Oranges may help regulate your digestion
